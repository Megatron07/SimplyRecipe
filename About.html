Better food for more people

Simply Recipes is here to help you cook delicious meals with less stress and more joy. We offer recipes and cooking advice for home cooks, by home cooks. Helping create “kitchen wins” is what we are all about.

Affordability:
Focusing on affordability. We are leaving no stone unturned when it comes to making food more affordable without compromising on the profitability of a given restaurant.
Accessibility:
Our delivery service is reaching more and more cities. We are actively growing our services - table reservation, food@work for corporate catering and are continuing to power Feeding India.
Quality:
We are committed to nurturing a neutral platform and are helping food establishments maintain high standards through Hyperpure. Food Hygiene Ratings is a coveted mark of quality among our restaurant partners
